<p><p>The horizontal metal baler market can be categorized into two types: manual and PLC automatic. In the manual market, the balers are operated by human efforts, requiring individuals to manually load the metal waste and operate the baler. On the other hand, in the PLC automatic market, the balers are equipped with advanced programmable logic controllers (PLCs) that automate the process. These balers do not require manual intervention and can perform tasks such as metal waste loading, compaction, and bale ejection automatically.</p></p>
