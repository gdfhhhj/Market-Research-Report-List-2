<p><ul><li>Liquid Membrane</li><li>PVC Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Calcium Ion Selective Electrodes (ISE) market consists of two types: Liquid Membrane and PVC Membrane. Liquid Membrane ISEs have an organic liquid layer that selectively interacts with calcium ions, generating an electrical potential. They provide accurate and precise measurements of calcium ion concentration. On the other hand, PVC Membrane ISEs use a solid polyvinyl chloride membrane that selectively binds with calcium ions, resulting in an electrical signal. They offer high sensitivity and are widely used in various applications requiring calcium ion detection, such as clinical diagnostics, pharmaceuticals, and environmental analysis.</p></p>
