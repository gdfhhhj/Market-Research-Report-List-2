<p><p>The malaria diagnostics market includes different types of tests used to detect the presence of malaria in individuals. Rapid Diagnostic Tests (RDT) are quick and easy to conduct, providing results within minutes. Molecular diagnostics involve the use of specialized equipment to detect specific genetic material of the malaria parasite in the blood samples. Microscopy is a traditional method where trained technicians examine blood smears under a microscope to identify the malaria parasites. Each diagnostic method offers its own advantages and limitations in terms of cost, accuracy, and practicality.</p></p>
