<p><p>Semiconductor etching equipment refers to the tools used in the semiconductor industry to remove unwanted materials from the surface of a semiconductor wafer. There are two main types of semiconductor etching equipment: dry etching equipment and wet etching equipment. Dry etching uses plasma or reactive gases to etch the material, while wet etching involves immersing the wafer in a chemical solution to dissolve the unwanted material. Both methods are crucial in the production of integrated circuits, as they allow for precise patterning and fabrication of semiconductor devices.</p></p>
