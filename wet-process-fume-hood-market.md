<p><p>The wet process fume hood market consists of two major types: horizontal laminar wet process fume hood and vertical laminar wet process fume hood. The horizontal laminar wet process fume hood is designed to provide a horizontal flow of filtered air, effectively controlling and removing hazardous fumes and particles during wet processes. On the other hand, the vertical laminar wet process fume hood offers a vertical flow of clean air, ensuring the safe handling of harmful substances in wet processes. These hoods are crucial in maintaining a safe and controlled environment within laboratories and other industrial settings.</p></p>
