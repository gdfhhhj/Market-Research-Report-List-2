<p><p>Air Inflation Flotation Cell is a type of machinery used for separating minerals from ore utilizing chemical flotation principles. It operates on the principle that certain minerals attach to air bubbles that are introduced into the cell's pulp, while other minerals do not. The air bubbles then rise to the surface of the cell and form a froth layer, which is subsequently scraped off to collect the valuable minerals.</p><p>The Air Inflation Flotation Cell Market is experiencing steady growth due to increasing demand for minerals, primarily from the mining industry.
