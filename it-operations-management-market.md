<p><p>IT Operations Management refers to the oversight and control of an organization's IT infrastructure and services. Within this market, three types can be identified. Service Mapping involves the identification and visualization of all IT components and their relationships to understand how they contribute to service delivery. Event Management focuses on monitoring and analyzing IT events, such as system failures or security breaches, to ensure prompt response and minimal downtime. Cloud Management involves managing and optimizing cloud-based services, including provisioning resources and monitoring performance, to enhance efficiency and meet organizational needs.</p></p>
