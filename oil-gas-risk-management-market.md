<p><strong>The Oil Gas Risk Management Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Initial Risk Management</li><li>Residual Risk Management</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Oil and gas risk management can be categorized into two types: initial risk management and residual risk management. Initial risk management involves identifying and assessing potential risks before any oil and gas activities begin. This includes evaluating geological and operational factors to anticipate potential hazards and develop appropriate risk mitigation strategies. Residual risk management, on the other hand, focuses on managing and mitigating risks that still exist after the oil and gas operations have commenced. This involves continuous monitoring, evaluation, and implementation of measures to reduce the impact of remaining risks.</p></p>
