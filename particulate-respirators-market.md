<p><ul><li>Valved Particulate Respirators</li><li>Unvalved Particulate Respirators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Particulate respirators are a type of personal protective equipment designed to filter out harmful particles present in the air. Valved particulate respirators have an exhalation valve, allowing for easier breathing and reducing moisture buildup within the mask. They are commonly used in industries such as construction and mining. On the other hand, unvalved particulate respirators lack the exhalation valve and are primarily used in healthcare settings where it is essential to prevent the spread of infectious diseases. Both types play a crucial role in protecting individuals from airborne hazards.</p></p>
