<p><ul><li>Radial Needle Cage</li><li>Thrust Needle Cage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The needle cage market includes two types: radial needle cage and thrust needle cage. Radial needle cages are used in various applications where radial forces are involved, such as in machinery and automotive components. They have a compact design and ensure smooth rotation and precise alignment. On the other hand, thrust needle cages are used to accommodate axial loads and provide low friction between moving parts. They are commonly used in automotive transmissions and steering systems, as well as in industrial machinery. Both types of needle cages play vital roles in enhancing the efficiency and performance of various systems.</p></p>
