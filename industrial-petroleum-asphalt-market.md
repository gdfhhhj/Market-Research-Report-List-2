<p><p>The industrial petroleum asphalt market can be classified into two types: liquid and semi-solid. Liquid asphalt refers to a form that is in a liquid state at room temperature. It is commonly used in road construction and as a binding agent in the manufacturing of asphalt shingles. On the other hand, semi-solid asphalt is a thicker consistency and has a higher softening point. It is primarily used in industrial applications such as roofing and waterproofing. These two types cater to different needs and have distinct characteristics, making them suitable for specific purposes within the petroleum asphalt market.</p></p>
