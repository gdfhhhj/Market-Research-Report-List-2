<p><p>The industrial rotary valves market consists of different types of valves such as butterfly valves, ball valves, and plug valves. Butterfly valves are quarter-turn valves with a disc that rotates to control the flow of liquids or gases. Ball valves have a spherical disc with a hole that rotates to regulate the flow. Plug valves have a cylindrical or conical plug that rotates to open or close the passage. These valves are commonly used in industrial processes to control the flow of fluids or gases in pipelines.</p></p>
