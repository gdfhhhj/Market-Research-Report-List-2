<p><p>Radio Frequency (RF) Relays are devices used to control the flow of signals in RF circuits. There are two main types of RF relays: DC type and AC type. </p><p>DC type relays are suitable for applications where low-frequency signals need to be switched, carrying direct current. They are commonly used in low-power RF systems. </p><p>AC type relays, on the other hand, are used for higher-frequency signals carrying alternating current. They are capable of handling higher power levels and are commonly found in industries such as telecommunications and broadcasting.</p></p>
