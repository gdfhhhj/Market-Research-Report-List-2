<p><p>Remote fiber laser welding systems are used for various types of welding processes, including conduction welding, laser spot welding, and deep penetration welding. Conduction welding involves the direct contact of the laser beam with the material to create a weld. Laser spot welding uses a focused laser beam to join small areas together. Deep penetration welding uses high-power lasers to achieve deep welds in thick materials. These different types of welding enable manufacturers to choose the most suitable method based on the specific requirements of their applications.</p></p>
