<p><p>The intraoral dental imaging system market consists of different types of dental X-rays, including bite wing X-rays, apical X-rays, and occlusal X-rays. Bite wing X-rays are used to detect cavities and assess the bone levels between teeth. Apical X-rays reveal the root structure and are commonly used in endodontic procedures. Occlusal X-rays capture a broad view of the upper and lower jaw to evaluate the overall structure and detect abnormalities. These imaging systems play a crucial role in diagnosing dental conditions and planning appropriate treatments.</p></p>
