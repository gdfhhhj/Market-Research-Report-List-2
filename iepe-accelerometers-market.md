<p><p>IEPE (Integrated Electronic Piezoelectric) accelerometers are a type of sensor widely used in various industries for measuring and analyzing vibration levels. The market for IEPE accelerometers can be segmented into two main types - Single Axis and Triaxial. Single Axis accelerometers measure vibration levels in one direction only, providing data on one specific axis of motion.
