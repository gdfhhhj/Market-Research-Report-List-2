<p><strong>The Brass Wires Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Alpha Brass</li><li>Alpha-beta Brass</li><li>Beta Brass</li><li>Gamma Brass</li><li>White Brass Wire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Brass wires are available in various types, each with its distinct properties. Alpha Brass wires are commonly used for electrical applications due to their high electrical conductivity. Alpha-beta Brass wires possess both alpha and beta-phase crystals, making them strong and corrosion-resistant, often used for mechanical purposes. Beta Brass wires are more ductile, suitable for forming intricate shapes. Gamma Brass wires have improved strength and are used in applications where high strength is required. White Brass wires, typically composed of copper, zinc, and nickel, offer a silver-like appearance, frequently used in decorative and jewelry making.</p></p>
