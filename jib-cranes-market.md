There are three market types of jib cranes: Free Standing Jib Cranes, Mast Type Jib Cranes, and Wall Mounted Jib Cranes. Free Standing Jib Cranes are standalone structures secured to the ground, providing flexibility in terms of positioning and installation. Mast Type Jib Cranes are attached to a support structure, typically a column, allowing for 360-degree rotation. Wall Mounted Jib Cranes are fixed to a wall or building structure, saving valuable floor space while still providing a wide range of movement.</p></p>
