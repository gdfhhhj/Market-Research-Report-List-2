<p><p>The Lightning Conductor market is divided into three main types. The Franklin Lightning Conductor is a traditional approach where a metal rod is installed at the highest point of a structure to attract lightning and redirect its energy safely into the ground. The Special Type Lightning Conductor incorporates additional features for specific applications. The Early Streamer Emission (ESE) Lightning Conductor is an advanced system that emits streamers earlier to intercept lightning, providing a wider protection radius. These variants cater to different needs and preferences in lightning protection.</p></p>
