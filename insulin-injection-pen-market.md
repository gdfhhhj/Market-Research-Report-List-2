<p><p>The insulin injection pen market consists of two main types: disposable insulin injection pens and reusable insulin injection pens. Disposable pens are designed for single-use and come pre-filled with insulin. Once the insulin is used up, the entire pen is discarded. Reusable pens, on the other hand, can be refilled with insulin cartridges multiple times. After each cartridge is emptied, a new one is inserted, making them a more cost-effective option for individuals who require regular insulin injections.</p></p>
