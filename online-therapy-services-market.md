CBT focuses on identifying and changing negative thought patterns and behaviors to promote positive outcomes. Psychodynamic Therapy delves into unconscious thoughts and childhood experiences to understand and address current issues. Person-centered Therapy emphasizes the therapist's empathy and unconditional positive regard to help individuals develop self-awareness and personal growth. These various approaches cater to individuals' unique needs and preferences, making online therapy services accessible to a wider range of clients.</p></p>
