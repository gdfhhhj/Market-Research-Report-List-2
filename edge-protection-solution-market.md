<p><p>Edge protection solutions are safety devices designed to prevent falls from edges and provide a secure working environment for workers on construction sites and high-rise buildings. These solutions include guard rails, handrails, edge protection barriers, toe boards, safety nets, and other devices that are installed along the edges of a structure to prevent accidents. Edge protection solutions are typically made of durable materials such as steel, aluminum, or plastic and are easy to install and dismantle.</p><p>The edge protection solution market is witnessing significant growth due to the increasing focus on worker safety and the stringent regulations imposed by various governments and regulatory bodies. The rising number of construction activities and infrastructure development projects across the globe is driving the demand for edge protection solutions. Additionally, the growing awareness about the benefits of edge protection solutions in preventing falls and accidents is further fueling the market growth.</p><p>The market is also experiencing several trends that are shaping the industry.
