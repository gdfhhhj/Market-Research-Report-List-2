<p><strong>The Wired X-Ray Flat Panel Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Conversion</li><li>Indirect Conversion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Wired X-Ray Flat Panel Detector market consists of two types: Direct Conversion and Indirect Conversion. Direct Conversion detectors convert X-ray radiation directly into an electrical signal using semiconductor materials like cadmium telluride or mercury iodide. They offer higher image quality and better spatial resolution. On the other hand, Indirect Conversion detectors use scintillation materials like cesium iodide to convert X-ray radiation into light, which is then converted into an electrical signal. They provide lower image quality but are more cost-effective.</p></p>
