<p><p>Industrial atmosphere furnaces are used in various industries for heat treatment processes like annealing, tempering, and hardening of metals. There are two main types: horizontal and vertical. Horizontal type furnaces have a chamber with a horizontal layout where the workpiece is placed horizontally for heat treatment. Vertical type furnaces, on the other hand, have a chamber with a vertical layout where the workpiece is placed vertically. Both types of furnaces provide controlled heating environments with specific atmospheres to achieve desired material properties.</p></p>
