<p><p>The spectrophotometers market comprises various types of spectrometry techniques used for analyzing different substances. UV-Visible spectrometry measures the absorption and transmission of UV and visible light to determine the concentration of substances. IR-Spectrometry measures the absorption and transmission of infrared light to identify and quantify chemical compounds. Nuclear Magnetic Resonance (NMR) uses the interaction between magnetic fields and molecular nuclei to provide structural and chemical information. Near Infrared Spectrometry analyzes the absorption of near-infrared light to quantify and identify substances. Raman Spectrometry measures the scattering of light to identify and analyze molecular structures.</p></p>
