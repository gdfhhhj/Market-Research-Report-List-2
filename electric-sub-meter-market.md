<p><ul><li>Single Electric Sub-meter</li><li>Three Electric Sub-meter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electric Sub-meter Market comprises two main types: Single Electric Sub-meter and Three Electric Sub-meter. A Single Electric Sub-meter measures the electricity consumption of a single unit, such as an individual apartment or a small office space. On the other hand, a Three Electric Sub-meter divides the electricity consumption into three different circuits, enabling better monitoring and analysis of electricity usage in larger premises, such as commercial buildings or multi-unit residential complexes. Both types play a crucial role in promoting energy efficiency and data management for billing purposes.</p></p>
