<p><p>Self-Reinforced Polypropylene (SRPP) is a composite material made from a combination of polypropylene (PP) fibers and a matrix of PP thermoplastic resin. SRPP offers a high strength-to-weight ratio, good impact resistance, and superior thermal stability.</p><p>The global market for SRPP is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period.
