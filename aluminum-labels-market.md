<p><p>The aluminum labels market consists of different types, each offering unique features. Natural aluminum labels maintain the original appearance of the metal, showcasing a sleek and minimalist look. Anodized aluminum labels undergo an electrochemical process, enhancing durability, corrosion resistance, and allowing for various color options. Coated aluminum labels have a protective layer applied, providing additional scratch and moisture resistance. Lastly, photosensitive anodized aluminum labels employ a light-sensitive method, enabling high-resolution graphics and text to be engraved onto the surface. These types cater to different preferences and applications in the aluminum labels industry.</p></p>
