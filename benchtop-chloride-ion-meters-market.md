<p><strong>The Benchtop Chloride Ion Meters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Input</li><li>Dual Input</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The benchtop chloride ion meters market consists of two types: single input and dual input meters. Single input meters are equipped with one channel for measuring chloride ions in a sample. These meters are suitable for applications requiring measurement of only one type of sample. On the other hand, dual input meters come with two channels, allowing simultaneous measurement of chloride ions in two separate samples. These meters are ideal for applications where multiple samples need to be tested simultaneously for chloride ion levels.</p></p>
