<p><p>An agricultural submersible pump is a device used for irrigation purposes in the agricultural sector. <p><p>The agricultural submersible pump market consists of various types such as oil-immersed type, water immersion type, dry type, and other markets. Oil-immersed type pumps rely on oil for cooling and lubrication, ensuring efficient operation. Water immersion type pumps, on the other hand, do not require oil for cooling, employing water instead.
