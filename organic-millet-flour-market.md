<p><strong>The Organic Millet Flour Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Whole Grain</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The organic millet flour market can be classified into two main types: whole grain and others. Whole grain millet flour refers to flour that is made from millet grains that have not undergone any processing or refining, thus retaining all parts of the grain, including the bran, germ, and endosperm. On the other hand, the "others" category includes millet flour that has been refined or processed, resulting in the removal of some or all of the bran and germ, leaving only the endosperm.</p></p>
