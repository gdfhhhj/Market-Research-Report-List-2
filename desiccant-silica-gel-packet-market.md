Firstly, the increasing demand for moisture control in various industries is driving the market. The pharmaceutical industry, in particular, requires strict moisture control to maintain the quality and efficacy of drugs. Additionally, the growing electronics industry, where moisture can cause significant damage to sensitive components, is also contributing to the market growth.</p><p>Moreover, the rising awareness about the benefits of using silica gel desiccant packets for moisture control is further fueling market growth. The packets are cost-effective, non-toxic, and reusable, which makes them a preferred choice among consumers.</p><p>Furthermore, the market is witnessing technological advancements, with manufacturers developing innovative packaging solutions for better moisture control.
