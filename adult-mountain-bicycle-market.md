<p><p>The adult mountain bicycle market can be categorized into four types: rigid, hardtail, softail, and full suspension. </p><p>1. Rigid mountain bikes have no suspension, making them suitable for smooth trails and roads.</p><p>2. Hardtail bikes have suspension forks in the front but no suspension in the rear, providing a more comfortable ride on uneven terrain.</p><p>3. Softail bikes feature a flexible frame that offers slight suspension at the rear, allowing for better shock absorption and improved control.</p><p>4. Full suspension bikes have both front and rear suspension, providing maximum shock absorption and stability on rough and rocky terrains.</p></p>
