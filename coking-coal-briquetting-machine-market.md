<p><p>Coking coal briquetting machines are used to convert coking coal fines into briquettes for easy handling, transportation, and storage. There are two types of machines available: mechanical and hydraulic. Mechanical machines use mechanical force to compress the coal fines into briquettes, while hydraulic machines use hydraulic force. Mechanical machines are generally cheaper and simpler, suitable for small-scale operations. Hydraulic machines, on the other hand, offer higher briquette quality and production capacity, making them ideal for large-scale manufacturing and commercial applications.</p></p>
