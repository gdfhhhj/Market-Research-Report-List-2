<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The HVAC contactor market comprises four main types: 1-pole, 2-pole, 3-pole, and 4-pole. The pole type indicates the number of electrical poles in the contactor. A 1-pole contactor has a single set of contacts, suitable for controlling one electrical circuit. A 2-pole contactor features two sets of contacts, enabling control over two circuits. Similarly, a 3-pole contactor has three sets of contacts, while a 4-pole contactor has four sets, enabling control over more electrical circuits. The different pole types cater to varying HVAC system requirements, offering flexibility in electrical connectivity options.</p></p>
