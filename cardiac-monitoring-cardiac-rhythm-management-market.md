<p><p>Cardiac Monitoring & Cardiac Rhythm Management Market includes various types of devices used for diagnosing and managing heart conditions. Electrocardiogram (ECG) Devices record electrical signals produced by the heart. Implantable Loop Recorders (ILRs) monitor heart rhythms over a long period by inserting under the skin. Cardiac Output Monitoring (COM) Devices measure blood flow from the heart. Event Monitors record heart's electrical activity during specific events. Pacemakers help regulate abnormal heartbeat. Cardiac Resynchronization Therapy (CRT) Devices coordinate heart's chambers. Defibrillators deliver electric shocks to restore regular heartbeat. External Defibrillators are portable versions. Ventricular Assist Devices (VADs) help pump blood in weakened hearts.</p></p>
