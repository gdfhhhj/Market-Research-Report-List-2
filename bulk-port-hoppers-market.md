<p><p>The bulk port hoppers market consists of three main types: static hoppers, rail hoppers, and wheeled hoppers. Static hoppers are stationary units that are typically installed at a fixed location in the port area. Rail hoppers, on the other hand, are designed to be transported on rail tracks and can be easily moved within the port. Wheeled hoppers are equipped with wheels, allowing them to be easily maneuvered and transported around the port using trucks or other vehicles. These different market types cater to various port requirements and offer flexibility in bulk material handling.</p></p>
