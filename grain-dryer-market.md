<p><p>Grain dryers are used in the agricultural sector to remove moisture from harvested grains, preventing spoilage and ensuring better storage. There are two main types of grain dryers: stationary and mobile. Stationary grain dryers are permanently installed on-site and are suitable for large-scale farming operations. Mobile grain dryers, on the other hand, are portable and can be easily moved from one location to another. They are commonly used by smaller farms or in areas where space is limited. Both types of grain dryers offer effective solutions for drying grains but cater to different farming needs.</p></p>
