<p><strong>The Precision Die Cutting Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flat Bed Die Cutting</li><li>Rotary Die Cutting</li><li>Waterjet Cutting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Precision die cutting is a cutting process used in various industries. The flat bed die cutting method involves placing the material on a flat surface and cutting it using a die. This method is suitable for thicker materials. Rotary die cutting involves the use of a cylindrical die to cut the material, suitable for continuous cutting of thin materials. Waterjet cutting uses a high-pressure jet of water to cut the material. It is ideal for fragile or heat-sensitive materials. Each method has its advantages and is used based on the specific requirements of the project.</p></p>
