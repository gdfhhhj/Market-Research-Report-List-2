<p><p>The marine steering gear system market consists of three main types: manual steering, hydraulic steering, and electric power steering. Manual steering requires the physical effort of the operator to turn the steering wheel and control the direction of the vessel. Hydraulic steering uses hydraulic fluid and pumps to assist with the steering, reducing the effort needed by the operator. Electric power steering utilizes an electric motor to assist with the steering, providing a more efficient and smoother steering experience. These different types cater to various needs and preferences in the marine industry.</p></p>
