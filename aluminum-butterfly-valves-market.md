<p><p>Aluminum butterfly valves are industrial valves commonly used to regulate or control the flow of fluids, gases, or slurries in various applications. They consist of a circular disc or plate that rotates on a central axis within the valve body. The disc is connected to a lever or actuator, which can be turned to open or close the valve. Aluminum butterfly valves are lightweight and durable, making them suitable for a range of industries including oil and gas, water treatment, chemical processing, and food and beverage.</p><p>The global aluminum butterfly valves market is expected to experience significant growth during the forecast period.
