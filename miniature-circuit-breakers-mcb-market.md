<p><p>Miniature Circuit Breakers (MCB) are devices used to protect electrical circuits from overcurrent or short circuits. They are available in different market types, including Type B, Type C, and Type D. Type B MCBs are suitable for everyday household applications with low inrush currents. Type C MCBs are designed for commercial and industrial uses with moderate inrush currents. Type D MCBs are ideal for heavy industrial applications with high inrush currents. Each type offers specific protection levels based on the characteristics of the connected electrical circuit, ensuring safe operation and preventing damage to electrical equipment.</p></p>
