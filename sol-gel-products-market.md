<p><p>Sol-gel products have various applications in different coating techniques. Spray coating involves the deposition of sol-gel materials using a spray gun, providing uniform and durable coatings. Capillary coating uses capillary action to draw sol-gel materials into porous substrates, creating protective layers. Spin coating entails spinning a substrate at high speeds while applying sol-gel materials, resulting in thin and even films. Flow coating spreads sol-gel solutions over a surface through controlled flow, ensuring consistent coverage. Roll coating involves applying sol-gel materials using a roller, suitable for large-scale production. Inkjet printing utilizes sol-gel inks to create precise patterns and designs.</p></p>
