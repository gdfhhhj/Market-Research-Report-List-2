<p><p>The Compressed Natural Gas (CNG) market can be categorized into three types based on the source of the gas: associated gas, non-associated gas, and unconventional sources. Associated gas is extracted along with crude oil production. Non-associated gas is found without crude oil and is extracted separately. Unconventional sources refer to gases obtained through methods such as coal-bed methane, shale gas, or tight gas. These different types of CNG sources offer varying potential in terms of availability, extraction techniques, and commercial viability.</p></p>
