<p><p>TOFD (Time-of-Flight Diffraction) Ultrasonic Testing Machines are used to detect and analyze defects in various materials. The market types for these machines include Electronic Scanning and Sector Scanning. Electronic Scanning machines use a linear array probe to scan the material in a straight line, providing detailed images of defects. Sector Scanning machines use a sectorial probe that produces an ultrasonic beam in a fan shape, allowing for the inspection of larger areas. Both types offer efficient and accurate defect detection capabilities for different testing needs.</p></p>
