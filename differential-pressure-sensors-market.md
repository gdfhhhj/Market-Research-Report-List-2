<p><p>Differential pressure sensors are used to measure the difference in pressure between two points. The digital type of differential pressure sensors provide a digital output signal, which can be easily processed by digital systems for monitoring and control purposes. On the other hand, analog type differential pressure sensors provide an analog output signal that can be directly connected to analog systems for measuring and controlling pressure. Both types have their advantages; digital sensors offer easier integration into digital systems, while analog sensors are simpler and cost-effective for analog setups.</p></p>
