<p><p>Drill chucks are devices used to hold drill bits securely in place on drilling machines. There are different types of drill chucks available in the market. Wrench drill chucks require the use of a wrench to tighten and loosen the chuck. Hand-tight drill chucks can be manually tightened and loosened by hand, making them convenient for quick bit changes. Self-tightening drill chucks automatically tighten and grip the drill bit as soon as the machine is switched on, providing a more secure hold during drilling operations.</p></p>
