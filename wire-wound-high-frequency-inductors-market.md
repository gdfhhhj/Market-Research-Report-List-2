<p><p>The wire wound high frequency inductors market is comprised of two main types: SMD (Surface Mount Device) Type and Plug-in Type. SMD Type inductors are designed to be mounted directly onto the surface of circuit boards, making them more space-efficient and suitable for high-density applications. On the other hand, Plug-in Type inductors are physically larger and have pins or leads that can be plugged into a circuit board. They are commonly used in applications where high power handling and ruggedness are required.</p></p>
