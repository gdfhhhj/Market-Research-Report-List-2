<p><p>The Photocatalytic Nanocoatings market can be categorized into two types: Water-Borne and Solvent-Borne coatings. Water-Borne coatings are those in which water is used as a medium for dispersing the nanocoatings, making them environmentally friendly and easy to apply. On the other hand, Solvent-Borne coatings utilize solvents as a dispersing medium, allowing for better adhesion and durability. Both types offer various advantages, such as self-cleaning properties and air purification abilities, making them highly suitable for applications in pollution control, construction, and automotive industries.</p></p>
