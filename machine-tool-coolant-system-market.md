<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Machine Tool Coolant System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Side-through Type</li><li>Center-through Type</li><li>Tool Holder Discharge Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The machine tool coolant system market consists of three types: Side-through type, Center-through type, and Tool Holder Discharge type. In the Side-through type, the coolant enters the side of the tool and flows through it. In the Center-through type, the coolant enters through the center of the tool and passes through it. The Tool Holder Discharge type expels the coolant through the tool holder. These systems play a crucial role in maintaining proper temperature, lubrication, and removing chips during machining processes to enhance efficiency and tool life.</p></p>
