<p><p>The Mobile MRI Scanner market can be categorized based on field strength into three types: Low Field Strength, Mid Field Strength, and High Field Strength. Low Field Strength scanners have magnetic fields below 0.5 Tesla, offering lower image quality but cost-effective solutions. Mid Field Strength scanners typically have magnetic fields ranging from 0.5 to 1.2 Tesla, striking a balance between image quality and affordability. High Field Strength scanners have magnetic fields above 1.2 Tesla, providing superior image quality but at a higher cost.</p></p>
