<p><p>Smart water network monitoring is a technology-driven system that enables the remote monitoring and management of water distribution networks. Factors driving this growth include the increasing need for efficient water management solutions, the aging and deteriorating water infrastructure in many countries, and stringent regulations concerning water conservation and leakage reduction.
