<p><p>Eye sphere implants are a type of medical device used in the reconstruction of eye cavities following the removal of an eye. They are categorized into two main market types: sterile orbital implants and non-sterile orbital implants. Sterile orbital implants are designed to be free from any microorganisms and are usually preferred in surgeries where the risk of infection is higher. On the other hand, non-sterile orbital implants do not undergo the same level of sterilization and may be used in specific cases where the risk of infection is lower. Both types serve the purpose of providing structural support and symmetry to the eye socket.</p></p>
