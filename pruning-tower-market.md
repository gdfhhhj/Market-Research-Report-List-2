<p><p>The Pruning Tower market consists of three main types: self-propelled pruning towers, trailed pruning towers, and other specialized options. Self-propelled pruning towers are equipped with their own engine and can easily navigate through orchards or vineyards, allowing for efficient and precise pruning. Trailed pruning towers, on the other hand, need to be towed by a tractor or another vehicle. These towers offer increased stability and can reach higher heights. The "other" category includes unique and specialized pruning towers designed for specific purposes or crop types, providing tailored solutions for different pruning needs.</p></p>
