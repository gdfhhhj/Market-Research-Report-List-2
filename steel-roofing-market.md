<p><p>The steel roofing market is divided into three main types: corrugated steel panels, steel shingles and shakes, and stone-coated steel tiles. Corrugated steel panels are affordable and commonly used for agricultural or industrial buildings. Steel shingles and shakes imitate the appearance of traditional roofing materials, offering a more residential appeal. Stone-coated steel tiles combine durability with an aesthetically pleasing look. Lastly, standing seam roofs are known for their sleek design and are often used in commercial or high-end residential applications.</p></p>
