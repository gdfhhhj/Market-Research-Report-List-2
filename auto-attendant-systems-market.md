<p><p>Auto attendant systems are communication solutions that automate call routing for businesses. There are two types of auto attendant systems: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based systems are hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet, offering scalability, flexibility, and reduced maintenance costs. On the other hand, on-premises systems are installed and maintained within the organization's infrastructure, providing more control and security but requiring higher upfront investments and ongoing management. Both types aim to enhance call handling efficiency and improve customer experience, albeit with different deployment and operational models.</p></p>
