<p><p>CPP (cast polypropylene) heat sealable film is used in various industries for packaging applications. There are three main types of CPP heat sealable films in the market. </p><p>1. General CPP Film: This is a regular CPP film that offers good clarity, excellent heat seal strength, and moisture barrier properties. It is commonly used in packaging snacks, confectionery, and other food products. </p><p>2. Metalized CPP Film: This type of film has a thin layer of metal coating, which provides excellent oxygen and moisture barrier properties. It is used for packaging products that require enhanced protection against light and air, such as coffee, pet food, and medical supplies.</p><p>3. Others: This category includes specialized CPP films with unique properties like high puncture resistance, anti-static qualities, or custom printing capabilities. These films are used in applications like electronics packaging, industrial products, and overwrapping.</p></p>
