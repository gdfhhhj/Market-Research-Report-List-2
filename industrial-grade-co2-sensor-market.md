<p><p>The industrial grade CO2 sensor market includes various types of sensors for accurately detecting carbon dioxide levels in industrial settings. NDIR (Non-Dispersive Infrared) CO2 sensors measure the concentration based on the absorption of infrared light. EC (Electrochemical) CO2 sensors use chemical reactions to detect CO2 levels. PAS (Photoacoustic Spectroscopy) CO2 sensors analyze the acoustic signal generated by CO2 molecules when exposed to light. Other types of sensors may include those using spectrometry or laser-based technology. Each type has its own advantages and suitability for specific industrial applications.</p></p>
