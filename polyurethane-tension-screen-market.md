<p><p>Polyurethane tension screens are used in the mining and aggregate industry for screening purposes. Transversal tension screens have wires running in a horizontal direction, allowing for efficient material separation. Longitudinal tension screens, on the other hand, have wires running in a vertical direction, providing superior screening performance. Both types offer high wear resistance, durability, and accurate sizing capabilities, making them popular choices in the market. These screens contribute to increased efficiency and productivity in various industries by allowing for effective material sorting and separation.</p></p>
