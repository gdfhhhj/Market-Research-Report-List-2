<p><p>UVB filters are commonly used in personal care products to protect the skin from harmful ultraviolet (UV) radiation. The market for UVB filters can be divided into two types: water phase and oil phase. Water phase filters are soluble in water and are typically used in products like lotions and creams. They provide effective UVB protection without leaving a greasy residue on the skin. On the other hand, oil phase filters are oil-soluble and are commonly found in products like sunscreens and lip balms. They provide long-lasting protection against UVB rays and are suitable for water-resistant formulations.</p></p>
