<p><p>The Excimer Laser Gases market entails two types of gases: Inert gas and Halogen gas. Inert gases such as Argon and Krypton do not chemically react with other substances, making them ideal for excimer lasers. They provide the necessary electron energy levels for emitting UV light. On the other hand, Halogen gases like Fluorine and Chlorine combine with the inert gas to create halide compounds, enhancing the laser's efficiency. Both gas types have significant applications in excimer laser technology, offering specific advantages depending on the required outcomes.</p></p>
