<p><ul><li>Screw Type</li><li>Manipulator Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The robotic camera market consists of two main types: screw type and manipulator type. The screw type cameras are designed with a motor-driven screw mechanism that enables the camera to move smoothly along a predefined track. This type of camera is usually installed in fixed locations such as stadiums or conference halls. On the other hand, manipulator type cameras use robotic arms to manipulate the camera's movement and positioning. These cameras are more versatile and can be used in various settings where manual adjustments and precise control are required.</p></p>
