<p><strong>The Enema Based Products Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cleansing</li><li>Carminative</li><li>Retention</li><li>Return-Flow</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Enema-based products market is categorized into four types: cleansing enemas, carminative enemas, retention enemas, and return-flow enemas. Cleansing enemas are used to cleanse the colon by stimulating bowel movements and removing waste material. Carminative enemas help relieve flatulence and abdominal discomfort by introducing gas into the colon. Retention enemas involve the introduction of therapeutic substances to be absorbed by the colon. Return-flow enemas are used to remove substances from the rectum and lower colon by repeatedly introducing and removing the enema solution.</p></p>
