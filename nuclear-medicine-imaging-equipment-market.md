<p><ul><li>Full Ring PET Scanners</li><li>Partial Ring PET Scanners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Nuclear Medicine Imaging Equipment Market includes two types of PET scanners: Full Ring and Partial Ring. Full Ring PET scanners consist of a circular arrangement of detector rings that provide a complete view of the patient's body during imaging. They offer higher sensitivity and image quality, making them suitable for various diagnostic applications. In contrast, Partial Ring PET scanners have a non-complete ring design, leading to reduced sensitivity and image quality. Despite their limitations, Partial Ring scanners are cost-effective options for certain clinical settings.</p></p>
