<p><p>The Scratch Testers market consists of three main types: Ordinary Scratch Tester, Linear Scratch Tester, and Multi Finger Scratch Tester. The Ordinary Scratch Tester evaluates the scratch resistance of surfaces using a single diamond stylus. The Linear Scratch Tester performs scratches with a linear motion to assess coating materials. The Multi Finger Scratch Tester simultaneously applies multiple scratches to measure the resistance of surfaces to damage. Each tester type serves different purposes in analyzing the scratch resistance properties of various materials and surfaces.</p></p>
