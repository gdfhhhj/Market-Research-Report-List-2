<p><p>The jewelry repair market encompasses various types of repairs, including ring sizing, broken shank repair, prong repair, loose gemstone repair, and chain and clasp repair. Ring sizing involves altering the size of a ring to fit the wearer's finger. Broken shank repair refers to fixing any damages to the band of the ring. Prong repair entails repairing or replacing the tiny metal prongs that hold gemstones in place. Loose gemstone repair involves securing loose stones back into their settings. Lastly, chain and clasp repair deals with fixing broken chains or clasps on necklaces or bracelets.</p></p>
