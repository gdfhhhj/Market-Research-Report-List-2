Mixed Based belts are made by combining fibers with various materials to enhance specific properties like flexibility or strength. Lastly, Resin Based carbon fiber belts are manufactured by impregnating the fibers with resin, offering improved strength and stiffness. These different types cater to varying needs and applications within the market.</p></p>
