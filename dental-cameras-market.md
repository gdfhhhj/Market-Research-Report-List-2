<p><p>The dental cameras market encompasses different types of cameras used in the field of dentistry. Dental intraoral cameras are small, handheld devices that are inserted into a patient's mouth to capture high-resolution images of teeth and gums. Extraoral cameras are larger devices that capture images of the entire face, jaws, and teeth. Dental digital cameras refer to cameras that can capture and store images digitally, often integrating with dental software for better data management. These cameras aid dentists in accurate diagnoses, treatment planning, and patient education.</p></p>
