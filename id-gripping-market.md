<p><p>The market for ID gripping can be broadly classified into three types: electric grippers, pneumatic grippers, and manual grippers. Electric grippers are powered by electricity and offer precise control with adjustable gripping force. Pneumatic grippers use compressed air to operate and are known for their fast and powerful gripping capabilities. Manual grippers, on the other hand, require human intervention to operate and are more commonly used in smaller-scale applications. Each market type caters to different industrial needs, providing versatility and flexibility in gripping solutions.</p></p>
