<p><p>Cermet potentiometers are electronic components used to measure and control electrical resistance in various applications. They can be classified into two types: single-turn cermet potentiometers and multi-turn cermet potentiometers. </p><p>Single-turn cermet potentiometers have a single rotating element that allows for variable resistance adjustment within a single rotation. These are commonly used in devices requiring simple resistance adjustments.</p><p>On the other hand, multi-turn cermet potentiometers have multiple rotating elements that provide precise resistance adjustment over multiple rotations. These are often used in applications requiring highly accurate resistance control, such as in scientific instruments or audio equipment.</p></p>
