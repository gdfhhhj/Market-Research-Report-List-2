<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The LED Driving Power Supply Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>External Power Supply</li><li>Built in Power Supply</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LED driving power supply market can be categorized into two main types: external power supply and built-in power supply. </p><p>External power supply refers to a separate unit that is connected externally to the LED device. It provides the required power and drives the LED efficiently. These are commonly used in applications where the LED devices are located at a distance from the power source.</p><p>On the other hand, built-in power supply refers to a power supply unit that is integrated or embedded within the LED device itself. This eliminates the need for an external power source as the LED device has a power supply unit built into its design. It is commonly used in applications where space or aesthetics are important factors.</p></p>
