<p><p>Touchless sensing refers to the technology that enables interaction with electronic devices without the need for physical contact. It allows healthcare professionals to access patient information and control medical devices without physically touching them, reducing the risk of infections.</p><p>The retail sector is also embracing touchless sensing technology to enhance the shopping experience. Touchless payment systems like contactless cards, mobile wallets, and QR code payments have gained significant traction. This technology enhances safety and convenience for drivers and passengers.</p><p>The increasing adoption of smartphones and other consumer electronics is driving the touchless sensing market further.
