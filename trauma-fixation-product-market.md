<p><p>The Trauma Fixation Product Market consists of various types of products used in the treatment of traumatic fractures. These include internal fixation groups such as plates, screws, nails, and other devices. Plates are metal plates that are attached to the surface of the bone using screws to stabilize the fracture. Screws are used to hold the bone fragments together. Nails are long metal rods inserted into the bone to provide stability. Other internal fixation devices include wires and pins. External fixation involves the use of devices outside the body, such as frames, to stabilize the fracture.</p></p>
