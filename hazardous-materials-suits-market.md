<p><p>The hazardous materials suits market consists of four main types: Level A, Level B, Level C, and Level D suits. Level A suits provide the highest level of protection and are fully encapsulated, including a self-contained breathing apparatus (SCBA) for respiratory protection. Level B suits offer similar protection but may have a lesser respiratory system. Level C suits are less protective as they do not provide respiratory protection but have other necessary equipment. Lastly, Level D suits are the least protective and mainly designed to protect against nuisance contamination.</p></p>
