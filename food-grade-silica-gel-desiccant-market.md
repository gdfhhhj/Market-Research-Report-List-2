<p><p>The food-grade silica gel desiccant market comprises three main types: fine-pored silica gel, mesoporous silica gel, and coarse-pore silica gel. Fine-pored silica gel offers high absorbency and is suitable for food packaging with low moisture contents. Mesoporous silica gel is known for its balanced absorption and desorption properties, making it ideal for products with medium moisture sensitivity. Coarse-pore silica gel, on the other hand, has a low absorption capacity but high stability, making it suitable for applications where high humidity levels are expected or prolonged exposure to moisture may occur.</p></p>
