<p><p>The Proximity Card Market consists of three main types: Low Frequency (LF) Cards, High Frequency (HF) Cards, and Ultra-High Frequency (UHF) Cards. LF cards operate at a frequency range of 125 kHz and are commonly used for access control, security systems, and identification purposes. HF cards operate at a frequency range of 13.56 MHz and are often used for applications like contactless payment, public transportation, and loyalty programs. UHF cards operate at a frequency range of 860-960 MHz and are primarily used for asset tracking, inventory management, and supply chain applications.</p></p>
