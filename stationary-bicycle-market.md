<p><p>The stationary bicycle market is comprised of two main types: recumbent exercise bikes and upright exercise bikes. Recumbent exercise bikes feature a reclined seating position and a larger seat, making them more comfortable and suitable for people with back or joint issues. Upright exercise bikes, on the other hand, mimic the feeling of riding a traditional bicycle, with a more upright position and smaller seat. They are preferred by those looking for a more intense workout and a closer simulation of outdoor cycling. Both types cater to different preferences and fitness goals.</p></p>
