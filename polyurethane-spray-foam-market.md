<p><p>The polyurethane spray foam market can be categorized into two types: rigid closed cell injection foam and open cell spray foam. Rigid closed cell injection foam is a dense and firm foam that creates a strong barrier against air leakage and moisture. It is commonly used for insulating walls, roofs, and foundations. On the other hand, open cell spray foam is a softer and more flexible foam that allows for air and moisture permeability. It is often used for soundproofing and insulation in areas like attics and basements.</p></p>
