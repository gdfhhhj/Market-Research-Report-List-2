<p><p>Plastic processing machinery refers to equipment used in the manufacturing process of plastic products. There are different types of machinery in this market. Injection Molding Machines (IMM) are used to produce plastic products by injecting melted plastic into a mold. Extrusion Machines are used to create continuous plastic products by pushing melted plastic through a die. Blow Molding Machines (BMM) are used to produce hollow plastic products by blowing air into a plastic tube. All these machines play a crucial role in the production of various plastic products for different industries.</p></p>
