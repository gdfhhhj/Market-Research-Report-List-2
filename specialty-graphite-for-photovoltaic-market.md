<p><p>Specialty graphite for the photovoltaic market includes various types such as isotropic graphite, extruded graphite, and others. Isotropic graphite is highly pure and exhibits consistent properties in all directions, making it suitable for applications requiring high thermal conductivity and electrical resistance. Extruded graphite is formed by forcing a mixture of graphite and binders through a shaped die, resulting in a material with controlled characteristics.
