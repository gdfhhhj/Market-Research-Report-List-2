<p><ul><li>Isokinetic Operation</li><li>Frequency Conversion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>There are different types of market operations for curved escalators, including isokinetic operation and frequency conversion. Isokinetic operation refers to a market where the equipment maintains a constant speed regardless of the load it carries, ensuring consistent performance. On the other hand, the frequency conversion market involves escalators that use variable frequency drives to change the speed of the escalator according to the load, resulting in energy savings and increased efficiency. Both market types aim to provide reliable and adaptable solutions for curved escalators.</p></p>
