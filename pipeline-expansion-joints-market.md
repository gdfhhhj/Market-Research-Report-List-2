<p><p>Pipeline expansion joints are devices used to compensate for thermal expansion, vibration, and misalignment in pipelines. Fabric expansion joints are made of high-temperature resistant fabrics and are ideal for gas and air applications. Metallic expansion joints are constructed from metal bellows and are suitable for high-pressure applications. Rubber expansion joints are made of synthetic elastomers and provide excellent resistance to chemicals and abrasion. Other types include refractory lined expansion joints for applications with high temperatures or corrosive gases. All these expansion joints play a vital role in maintaining the integrity and performance of pipeline systems.</p></p>
