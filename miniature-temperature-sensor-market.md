<p><strong>The Miniature Temperature Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pyroelectric Temperature Sensor</li><li>PN junction temperature Sensor</li><li>IC Temperature Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Miniature Temperature Sensor market offers various types of sensors for temperature measurement. Pyroelectric temperature sensors generate electric charge in response to temperature changes, enabling them to detect infrared radiation. PN junction temperature sensors use the voltage change across a PN junction of a diode to measure temperature accurately. IC temperature sensors integrate temperature sensing elements with signal conditioning and offer high accuracy and resolution for temperature measurement. These different types cater to the diverse needs of industries and applications requiring precise temperature monitoring.</p></p>
