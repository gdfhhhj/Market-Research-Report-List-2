<p><p>The underground drilling equipment market consists of three main types: single boom drilling rigs, double boom drilling rigs, and multi boom drilling rigs. Single boom drilling rigs have a single arm, making them suitable for small-scale operations. Double boom drilling rigs have two arms and are versatile, capable of drilling in different directions. Multi boom drilling rigs have multiple arms and are used for large-scale projects, allowing for simultaneous drilling in various directions. These different types cater to the varying needs and scales of underground drilling operations.</p></p>
