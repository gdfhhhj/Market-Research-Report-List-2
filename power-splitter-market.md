<p><p>The power splitter market offers different types of products to distribute the power signal. A 2-way splitter divides the signal into two equal parts, allowing connection to two devices simultaneously. Similarly, a 3-way splitter splits the signal into three outputs, enabling connections to three devices. A 4-way splitter extends the distribution to four outputs. Apart from these common types, the market also offers other variations like 6-way, 8-way, and higher, which allow more devices to be connected efficiently. These power splitters cater to various needs and enable users to distribute power signals conveniently.</p></p>
