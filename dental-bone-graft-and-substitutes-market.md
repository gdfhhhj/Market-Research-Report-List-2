<p><p>The Dental Bone Graft and Substitutes market comprises different types of graft materials used in dental procedures. Allografts are grafts obtained from human donors, Autografts are grafts taken from the patient's own body, Xenografts are grafts derived from animal sources, and Synthetic grafts are man-made materials. These graft materials are used to augment or replace missing or damaged bone tissue in dental surgeries. Each type of graft material has its own advantages and limitations, and the market for these materials caters to the varying needs and preferences of dental professionals.</p></p>
