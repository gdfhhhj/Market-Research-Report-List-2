<p><p>IEC filter market types refer to different configurations of filters used in electrical systems to reduce electromagnetic interference (EMI). A 1-pole filter consists of a single filtering component, suitable for applications where only one phase needs filtering. A 2-pole filter has two components for filtering two phases. A "none" market type means no filter is present, which might be applicable in situations where EMI is not a concern or has been addressed differently. "Others" category represents additional filter configurations or variations not specifically classified in the 1 or 2 pole types, providing more customized filtering options for specific requirements.</p></p>
