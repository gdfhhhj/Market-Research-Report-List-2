Digital BOD sensors provide precise and accurate measurements, converting the analog signals into digital data for easy interpretation and integration with other systems. Analog BOD sensors, on the other hand, provide continuous readings in a voltage range, relying on signal processing to interpret the information. Both types have their specific advantages and applications within environmental monitoring and water treatment industries.</p></p>
