<p><p>The Crossband Coupler market consists of three different types: Tower-Mounted, Rack-Mounted, and Stand Alone. The Tower-Mounted crossband couplers are designed to be installed directly onto telecommunication towers, providing efficient signal distribution. Rack-Mounted crossband couplers are intended for installation in server racks, enabling easy integration into existing communication systems. Stand Alone crossband couplers are independent units that can be placed in various locations, allowing for flexibility in signal distribution. Each type offers distinct advantages depending on installation requirements, making crossband couplers suitable for a wide range of applications in telecommunications.</p></p>
