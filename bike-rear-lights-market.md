<p><p>The bike rear lights market consists of two main types: self-generating lights and battery-powered lights. Self-generating lights are designed to harness the energy produced by the bike's movement to power the lights, ensuring they never run out of battery. On the other hand, battery-powered lights rely on replaceable or rechargeable batteries for operation, providing convenience and flexibility. Both types offer different benefits, with self-generating lights being eco-friendly and cost-effective in the long term, while battery-powered lights are more easily adjustable and can provide brighter illumination.</p></p>
