<p><p>Molded fiber packaging for food service can be classified into two types: wood pulp based and non-wood pulp based. Wood pulp based packaging is made from renewable wood fibers, such as recycled paper materials. It provides good strength and insulation properties, making it suitable for carrying hot or cold food items. On the other hand, non-wood pulp based packaging is made from alternative fibers like sugarcane or bamboo. It offers similar performance to wood pulp based packaging but with the added benefit of being more sustainable and eco-friendly.</p></p>
