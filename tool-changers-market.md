<p><p>Tool changers are devices used in various industries to quickly and efficiently switch tools on machines. There are two main types of tool changers: manual and automatic. Manual tool changers require the operator to physically remove and replace tools, whereas automatic tool changers use robotic or mechanical systems to swap tools without human intervention. Manual tool changers are typically more cost-effective but may be slower and less precise than automatic ones. Automatic tool changers offer increased speed and accuracy but can be more expensive. Both types are used based on specific needs and applications.</p></p>
