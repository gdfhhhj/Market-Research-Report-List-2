<p><p>Dosimetry Services Market Types refers to different technologies used in measuring and monitoring radiation exposure. OSL (Optically Stimulated Luminescence) Technology employs specially designed crystals that emit light when exposed to radiation, providing accurate dose measurements. TLD (Thermoluminescent Dosimetry) Technology utilizes small crystals that store energy when exposed to radiation, measuring the released energy as a dose. CR-39 Technology involves using a plastic material that tracks radiation exposure by forming visible tracks on its surface. These technologies contribute to the efficient and precise assessment of radiation levels for occupational and personal safety purposes.</p></p>
