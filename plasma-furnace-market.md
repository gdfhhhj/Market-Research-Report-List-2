<p><p>Plasma furnace is a type of furnace that utilizes plasma, a highly ionized gas, to melt or process various materials. There are different types of plasma furnaces in the market, including ceramic crucible plasma furnace, crystallizer plasma furnace, and scull plasma furnace. The ceramic crucible plasma furnace uses a ceramic crucible to contain and heat the material. The crystallizer plasma furnace is specifically designed for the solidification or crystallization of materials. Lastly, the scull plasma furnace focuses on the removal of impurities or unwanted substances from the material being processed.</p></p>
