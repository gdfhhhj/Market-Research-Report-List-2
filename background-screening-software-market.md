<p><p>Background screening software is a specialized solution used by organizations to conduct comprehensive screenings of job applicants, contractors, and other individuals to verify their qualifications, credentials, employment history, criminal records, and other relevant information. These software systems leverage artificial intelligence and machine learning algorithms to analyze vast amounts of data and provide actionable insights. This enables organizations to streamline their screening processes, save time, and improve accuracy.</p><p>Additionally, the growing adoption of cloud-based solutions is driving market growth.
