<p><strong>The Commercial Label Printers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inkjet</li><li>Laser</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Commercial label printers are available in various types, including inkjet, laser, and others. Inkjet printers utilize liquid ink droplets to create images on labels, offering high-resolution printing and vibrant colors. Laser printers, on the other hand, use a laser beam to heat the label's surface, resulting in precise and durable prints. Lastly, the "others" category encompasses different technologies like thermal transfer printing, which transfers ink onto the labels using heat, and direct thermal printing, where heat activates a chemical coating on the label to create images.</p></p>
