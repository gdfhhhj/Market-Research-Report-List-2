<p><p>PVC wall cladding is categorized into two main types: external and internal. External PVC wall cladding is designed to be installed on the exterior surfaces of buildings, providing protection against weather conditions, UV radiation, and other external elements. It enhances the appearance of the building and offers durability and low maintenance. On the other hand, internal PVC wall cladding is meant to be used indoors, often in areas prone to moisture like kitchens and bathrooms. It provides an easy-to-clean, hygienic, and aesthetically pleasing solution, protecting walls and preventing moisture-related issues.</p></p>
