<p><p>The lightweight steel frames market consists of three main types: iron skeleton, endurance wall, and abutment structure. The iron skeleton frames are made of steel beams and columns, providing a strong and durable structure. Endurance walls are steel panels that offer resistance to extreme weather conditions and impact. Abutment structures include steel brackets and supports, providing stability and strength to buildings. These various types of lightweight steel frames offer different benefits, such as high strength, durability, and protection, making them suitable for various construction applications.</p></p>
