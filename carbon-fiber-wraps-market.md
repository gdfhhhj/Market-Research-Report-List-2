<p><p>Carbon fiber wraps are widely used in various industries due to their high strength-to-weight ratio. There are three main types of carbon fiber wraps based on the raw materials used in their production. Rayon-based wraps are derived from viscose rayon and offer good flexibility. Pitch-based wraps are made from petroleum pitch and exhibit high thermal conductivity. Pan-based wraps, produced from polyacrylonitrile, are the most popular type commercially, providing excellent strength and stiffness. These different types cater to diverse applications, such as aerospace, automotive, and sporting goods.</p></p>
