<p><p>The Stainless Steel Commercial Kitchen Knife market is divided into two main types: Stamped Blade Products and Forged Blade Products. Stamped Blade Products are made by cutting the blade shape out of a flat sheet of steel, resulting in a lighter and less expensive knife. On the other hand, Forged Blade Products are created by heating and shaping a single piece of steel through intense pressure, resulting in a heavier and more durable knife. Both types offer different benefits and cater to various preferences in the commercial kitchen industry.</p></p>
