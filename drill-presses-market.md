<p><p>Drill presses are categorized into two main types: bench top drill presses and floor standing drill presses. Bench top drill presses are compact and designed to be mounted on a workbench or table. They are suitable for smaller projects and offer versatility and ease of use. On the other hand, floor standing drill presses are larger and sturdier machines that are suitable for heavy-duty tasks. They are designed to be freestanding and offer more power and precision for professional workshops or industrial settings.</p></p>
