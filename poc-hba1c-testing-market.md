<p><p>POC HbA1C testing market is categorized into different types based on the technology used for testing. Immunoassay is a widely used method that measures the concentration of HbA1C using antibodies. Boronate affinity utilizes the high affinity of boronic acid for glycated hemoglobin to measure the HbA1C levels. There are also other types of technologies used in the market, which may include enzymatic methods or combination methods using multiple technologies. These different market types offer diverse options for healthcare providers to accurately measure and monitor HbA1C levels in a point-of-care setting.</p></p>
