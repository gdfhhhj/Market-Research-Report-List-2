<p><p>The Power Management IC (PMIC) market consists of various types of integrated circuits that perform specific functions related to power management in electronic devices. Voltage regulators regulate and stabilize the voltage supplied to a device. Supervisory circuits monitor the power supply and provide voltage monitoring and reset functions. Gate driver ICs control the power switches in devices. Battery management ICs manage and control the charging/discharging of batteries. Voltage references provide a stable voltage for accuracy in circuit operation. LED lighting driver ICs regulate the current supplied to LED lights for proper functioning.</p></p>
