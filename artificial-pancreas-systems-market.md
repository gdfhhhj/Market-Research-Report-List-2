<p><strong>The Artificial Pancreas Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Control to Range (CTR) System</li><li>Control to Target (CTT) System</li><li>Threshold Suspended Device</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Artificial Pancreas Systems (APS) include different types such as Control to Range (CTR) System, Control to Target (CTT) System, and Threshold Suspended Device market. The CTR system maintains blood glucose levels within a specified range, adjusting insulin delivery as needed. The CTT system sets a specific target value for blood glucose, ensuring insulin is delivered accordingly. Threshold Suspended Device is designed to suspend insulin delivery temporarily when blood glucose levels fall below a predetermined threshold. These APS technologies aim to automate glucose control and improve the management of diabetes.</p></p>
