<p><p>Prosthetic sockets are a crucial part of prosthetic limbs. The transfemoral socket is designed for individuals who have undergone above-knee amputations. It is custom-made to fit securely on the residual limb, providing stability and allowing users to transfer weight effectively. On the other hand, the leg socket is designed for individuals who have undergone below-knee amputations. It is crafted to fit snugly on the residual limb, offering support and comfort during ambulation. Both types of sockets play a vital role in facilitating mobility for amputees.</p></p>
