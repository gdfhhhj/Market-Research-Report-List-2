<p><p>Ethanol fuel testing market involves various types of analysis to ensure the quality and compliance of ethanol fuel. Ethanol purity testing checks the concentration of ethanol in the fuel. Methanol content analysis detects the presence of methanol, which is a contaminant. Solvent washed gums testing measures the amount of insoluble residues in the fuel. Water content analysis evaluates the moisture level in the fuel. Denaturant content analysis determines the presence of denaturants, which are added to make the ethanol unfit for human consumption. Inorganic chloride content analysis detects the level of chloride ions in the fuel, which can be harmful to engines.</p></p>
