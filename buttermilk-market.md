<p><p>Buttermilk comes in two main market types: liquid buttermilk and buttermilk powder. Liquid buttermilk is the traditional form which is obtained as a by-product of butter-making process. It is commonly used in baking and cooking. On the other hand, buttermilk powder is a dehydrated form of liquid buttermilk, where the liquid is evaporated and converted into powder. It has a longer shelf life and is more convenient for storage and transportation. Both liquid buttermilk and buttermilk powder serve as popular ingredients in various food products and recipes.</p></p>
