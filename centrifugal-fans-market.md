<p><strong>The Centrifugal Fans Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Forward</li><li>Backward</li><li>Radial</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Centrifugal fans are classified into three types based on the direction of airflow and the orientation of the fan blades - forward, backward, and radial. </p><p>Forward curved blades in forward fans are designed to move a large volume of air at low pressure, making them energy-efficient for applications such as ventilation and air conditioning systems. </p><p>Backward curved blades in backward fans create a high-pressure airflow with low noise levels, making them suitable for applications like air filtration and industrial processes. </p><p>Radial fans have straight blades that provide a medium level of pressure and airflow, making them versatile for various applications requiring a moderate flow rate and pressure.</p></p>
