<p><p>The solid rubber tires market consists of two types: cylindrical solid tires and oblique bottom solid tires. Cylindrical solid tires are round in shape and have a uniform diameter throughout. They offer enhanced stability and durability, making them ideal for heavy-duty vehicles like forklifts and industrial equipment. On the other hand, oblique bottom solid tires have a unique design with a sloping bottom surface. This design provides increased traction and better performance on uneven terrains, making them suitable for off-road vehicles and outdoor equipment. Both types cater to specific needs in the solid rubber tires market.</p></p>
