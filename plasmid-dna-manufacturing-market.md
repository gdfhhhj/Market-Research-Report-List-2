GMP Grade Plasmid DNA refers to plasmid DNA manufactured according to Good Manufacturing Practices guidelines, ensuring it meets strict quality standards for use in clinical trials and commercial applications. Non-GMP Grade Plasmid DNA refers to plasmid DNA manufactured without adhering to GMP guidelines and is primarily used for preclinical research or non-regulated applications.</p></p>
