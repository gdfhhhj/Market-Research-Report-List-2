<p><p>Soluble rosin flux is a type of flux used in soldering processes to remove oxides and contaminants from metal surfaces, enabling better solder adhesion. It is available in different forms, namely liquid, paste, and solid. Liquid flux is a flowable solution, easily applied using brushes or sprays. Paste flux has a thicker consistency and is commonly supplied in syringes for precise application. Solid flux is in a solid state and requires activation by heat before use. Each form offers advantages depending on the specific soldering application and desired convenience of use.</p></p>
