<p><ul><li>Plate Heat Exchangers</li><li>Shell or Tube Heat Exchangers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pasteurisers are used in various industries for heat treatment of liquids, such as milk, juice, and beer, to eliminate harmful microorganisms while preserving flavor and nutritional value. There are two main types: plate heat exchangers and shell or tube heat exchangers. Plate heat exchangers use a series of metal plates with passages for the liquid to flow through, maximizing heat transfer. Shell or tube heat exchangers consist of a shell with tubes inside, facilitating heat exchange between the liquid and a hot medium. Both types have their advantages and are widely used in the pasteuriser market.</p></p>
