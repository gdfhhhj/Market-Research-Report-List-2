<p><p>The glass-reinforced PP compounds market consists of two main types: short glass fibers and long glass fibers. Short glass fibers are typically less than 3 mm in length and are used to enhance the mechanical strength and dimensional stability of the PP compound. On the other hand, long glass fibers are usually between 6 and 25 mm in length and provide improved strength, stiffness, and impact resistance to the PP compound. Both types of glass fibers play a crucial role in enhancing the overall performance and durability of PP compounds in various applications.</p></p>
