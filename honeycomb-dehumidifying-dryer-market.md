<p><p>The Honeycomb Dehumidifying Dryer market has two types: Three in One and All in One. Three in One dehumidifying dryers are equipped with three functions: drying, dehumidifying, and conveying. They are widely used in the plastic industry to effectively remove moisture from materials during the drying process. On the other hand, All in One dehumidifying dryers combine the drying, dehumidifying, and conveying functions into a single unit. They are compact and efficient, offering a space-saving solution for industries that require all three functions simultaneously.</p></p>
