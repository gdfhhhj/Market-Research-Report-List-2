The absorption exhaust muffler is designed to absorb sound waves through acoustic packing material, while the reactive exhaust muffler uses chambers and tubes to reflect and cancel out sound waves. Other types of mufflers in the market include combination mufflers that use a combination of absorption and reflection techniques, and chambered mufflers that use chambers to create sound cancellation. These mufflers aim to minimize noise and improve overall engine performance.</p></p>
