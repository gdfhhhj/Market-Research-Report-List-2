<p><p>The 3D printing glasses frame market offers different types of materials for manufacturing eyewear. Polyamide 12, also known as Nylon, is a popular material due to its durability, flexibility, and lightweight properties. Photopolymers are used in Stereolithography (SLA) 3D printing technology, providing higher precision and surface finish. Filaments, made from various materials like PLA or ABS, are commonly used in Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM) printers, offering a wide range of colors and finishes. Metals, such as titanium or stainless steel, are employed for their strength and premium quality in metal additive manufacturing processes.</p></p>
