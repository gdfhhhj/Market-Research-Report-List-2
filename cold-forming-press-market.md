<p><ul><li>Electric</li><li>Hydraulic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Cold Forming Press market includes two primary types: Electric and Hydraulic presses. Electric cold forming presses utilize an electric motor to generate the required force for the forming process. These presses are easier to operate, offer precise control over the force, and have low maintenance requirements. On the other hand, Hydraulic cold forming presses use hydraulic systems to generate force, offering higher power and capacity for heavy-duty applications. They are suitable for handling larger workpieces but may require more maintenance compared to electric presses.</p></p>
