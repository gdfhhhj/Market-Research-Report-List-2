The pandemic has highlighted the importance of a robust and resilient healthcare supply chain to ensure the availability of essential medical supplies. <p><p>Healthcare supply chain software market can be categorized into two types: On-premise and Cloud-based. On-premise software is installed and operated on local servers within the healthcare provider's premises. It requires dedicated IT infrastructure, maintenance, and frequently involves higher upfront costs. On the other hand, cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet. It offers scalability, flexibility, lower upfront costs, and easier updates. Cloud-based solutions are gaining popularity due to their accessibility, cost-effectiveness, and ability to provide real-time data access on various devices.</p></p>
