<p><p>The electric power transmission transformer market can be classified into three types based on voltage levels. Low voltage transformers are designed to step down voltage from the distribution network to power appliances and devices in households and commercial buildings. Medium voltage transformers are used to transmit power over longer distances and are typically found in industrial settings. High voltage transformers are crucial for long-distance transmission, converting power to extremely high voltages for efficient transmission over power grids. Each transformer type serves a specific purpose in the electric power transmission system.</p></p>
