<p><strong>The Vegetative Roof System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Extensive Type</li><li>Semi-intensive Type</li><li>Intensive Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vegetative roof systems are roofing structures that incorporate vegetation, which provide various environmental and aesthetic benefits. The extensive type is characterized by a shallow growing media, limited maintenance, and low vegetation diversity, making it suitable for lightweight roofs. Semi-intensive type roofs have a deeper growing media, greater vegetation diversity, and require moderate maintenance. They offer a balance between extensive and intensive types. Lastly, intensive type roofs have the deepest growing media, allow for diverse vegetation, and necessitate high maintenance. They are commonly used in urban areas for recreational purposes or agricultural cultivation.</p></p>
