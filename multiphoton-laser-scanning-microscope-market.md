<p><p>The multiphoton laser scanning microscope market has various types of systems, including upright microscope systems, gantry microscope systems, and inverted microscope systems. </p><p>Upright microscope systems are designed with the objective lens located above the sample, making them suitable for imaging thick tissues. </p><p>Gantry microscope systems have a larger scanning area and are commonly used for imaging whole organs or small animals. </p><p>Inverted microscope systems have the objective lens located below the sample, making them ideal for imaging living cells or tissues in culture dishes.</p></p>
