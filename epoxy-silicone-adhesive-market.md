<p><p>Epoxy silicone adhesive is a versatile product used in various industries. In terms of market types, it can be categorized into PSA (Pressure Sensitive Adhesive) and Non-PSA markets. PSA epoxy silicone adhesive is designed to form an instant bond when applied to a surface with light pressure. It is commonly used in applications such as labels, tapes, and stickers due to its easy peel-off nature. On the other hand, Non-PSA epoxy silicone adhesive requires additional curing or bonding processes for it to effectively adhere to surfaces. It is typically used in more permanent bonding applications, such as electronic components or structural assemblies.</p></p>
