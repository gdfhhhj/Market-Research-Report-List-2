<p><p>Shrink bags for the fresh food market come in different types, such as EVOH based, PVDC based, and others. EVOH based shrink bags refer to those that are made with a material called ethylene vinyl alcohol, which provides excellent oxygen barrier properties, keeping the food fresh for a longer period. PVDC based shrink bags, on the other hand, are made with a material called polyvinylidene chloride, which also offers good oxygen barrier properties. The "others" category includes shrink bags made with different materials that may have varying oxygen barrier capabilities. These different types cater to different packaging needs in the fresh food market.</p></p>
