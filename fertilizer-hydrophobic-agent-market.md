<p><p>A fertilizer hydrophobic agent is a substance used in agricultural practices to improve the efficiency of fertilizers by preventing the absorption of water. These agents are usually added to the surface of granular fertilizers to form a water-repellent barrier, which allows the fertilizers to resist moisture and adhere to the targeted areas during application. This ensures that the nutrients are released gradually and efficiently to the plants' roots, minimizing wastage and maximizing the effectiveness of the fertilizers.</p><p>The future outlook of the fertilizer hydrophobic agent market appears promising.
