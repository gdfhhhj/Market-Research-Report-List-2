<p><p>The reusable latex gloves market is classified into four types: natural rubber, neoprene, nitrile rubber, and other materials. Natural rubber gloves are made from the sap of rubber trees and are known for their elasticity and durability. Neoprene gloves are synthetic rubber gloves resistant to heat and chemicals, commonly used in industrial settings. Nitrile rubber gloves are made from a synthetic material, offering excellent chemical resistance and puncture protection. Lastly, the "other" category includes gloves made from different materials such as vinyl or PVC, catering to specific needs of consumers.</p></p>
