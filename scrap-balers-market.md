<p><p>Scrap balers are machines used to compress and package waste materials for efficient storage, transportation, and recycling. Vertical balers have a vertical orientation, with materials being fed into the top and compressed downward. They are suitable for smaller volumes of waste. Horizontal balers, on the other hand, have a horizontal orientation where materials are fed from the side and compacted horizontally. These balers are ideal for handling larger quantities of waste. Both types serve the purpose of reducing waste volume and facilitating its proper disposal or recycling.</p></p>
