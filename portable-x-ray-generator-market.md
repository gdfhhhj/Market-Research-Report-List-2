<p><p>The Portable X-ray Generator market consists of two main types: High Frequency X-ray Generators and Low Frequency X-ray Generators. High Frequency X-ray Generators use high voltage in the range of 50-150 kHz, delivering better image quality, faster exposure times, and improved efficiency. They are commonly used in medical and industrial applications. Low Frequency X-ray Generators, on the other hand, operate at lower frequencies below 50 kHz, providing lower image quality and longer exposure times. They are often used in less demanding environments such as veterinary clinics and non-destructive testing.</p></p>
