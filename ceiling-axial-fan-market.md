<p><p>The Ceiling Axial Fan market can be categorized into two types: AC Axial Fans and DC Axial Fans. AC Axial Fans are powered by AC (alternating current) electrical energy, commonly found in households and businesses. On the other hand, DC Axial Fans use DC (direct current) electrical energy, usually generated by batteries or renewable energy sources. Both types of fans are designed to move air in a parallel direction to the shaft, making them ideal for ventilation, cooling, and air circulation applications.</p></p>
