<p><p>The Carbon Fiber Geogrid market consists of two main types - Biaxial Geogrids and Uniaxial Geogrids. Biaxial Geogrids are designed to provide strength in both the longitudinal and transverse directions, making them ideal for applications where load distribution is required in multiple directions. On the other hand, Uniaxial Geogrids offer strength primarily in one direction, making them suitable for applications where load-bearing capacity is needed in a specific direction. Both types of geogrids play a crucial role in infrastructure and construction projects, providing stability and reinforcement to soil and aggregate materials.</p></p>
