<p><p>Desiccant adsorbents are materials used to remove moisture or water vapor from the air or gas. The market for desiccant adsorbents can be divided into different types, including molecular sieve, silica gel, activated alumina, and others. Molecular sieves are crystalline materials with uniform pores that can selectively adsorb molecules based on their size. Silica gel is a granular form of silicon dioxide that can adsorb moisture due to its high surface area. Activated alumina is a porous form of aluminum oxide that can adsorb water vapor effectively. The "others" category includes desiccants like zeolites and clay minerals.</p></p>
