<p><p>The liquid packaging market is divided into two types: flexible and rigid packaging. It offers convenience in transportation and storage due to its lightweight and flexible nature. On the other hand, rigid liquid packaging is made of materials like glass or metal that provide a more durable and sturdy packaging solution. Rigid packaging is commonly used for premium or high-end liquid products.</p></p>
