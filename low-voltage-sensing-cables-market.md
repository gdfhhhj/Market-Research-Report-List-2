<p><p>Low Voltage Sensing Cables are available in two types: Single-mode Fiber and Multi-mode Fiber. Single-mode Fiber is designed for long-distance transmission and has a smaller core size, enabling transmission of a single light signal. It is suitable for applications that require high speed and accuracy. On the other hand, Multi-mode Fiber has a larger core size and transmits multiple light signals simultaneously. It is more cost-effective for short-distance transmissions but may have limitations in terms of speed and distance. Both types cater to different needs in the low voltage sensing market.</p></p>
