<p><ul><li>Bridge</li><li>Portal</li><li>Folding Arm</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electromagnetic crane market is divided into three types: bridge, portal, and folding arm cranes. Bridge cranes consist of a horizontal beam that moves along a track, allowing the crane to cover a wide area. Portal cranes are similar but have supporting legs on each side, providing increased stability for heavier loads. Folding arm cranes are characterized by their extendable arms, which can reach difficult-to-access areas. These different types cater to various industrial needs, providing flexibility and efficiency in handling heavy objects.</p></p>
