<p><p>The gas sampling pump market can be categorized into two types: desktop and handheld. Desktop gas sampling pumps are usually larger in size and are designed to be placed on a table or workbench. They are ideal for stationary gas sampling applications where portability is not a concern. On the other hand, handheld gas sampling pumps are smaller and portable, allowing users to easily move them around various locations for on-the-go gas sampling tasks. Both types serve the purpose of extracting gas samples for analysis but differ in terms of size and portability.</p></p>
