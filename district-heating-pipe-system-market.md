<p><p>The District Heating Pipe System Market includes two main types: Hot Water Heating System and Steam Heating System. Hot Water Heating System involves the use of hot water as a heat source to transfer warmth to buildings through pipes. Steam Heating System, on the other hand, utilizes steam generated from a central plant to distribute heat through pipes. Both systems provide efficient and cost-effective heating solutions for large-scale applications in urban areas, ensuring a reliable supply of warmth to buildings connected to the district heating network.</p></p>
