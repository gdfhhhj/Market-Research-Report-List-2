<p><p>Grain handling systems refer to equipment used in the agricultural industry to transport and handle grain efficiently. There are several types of grain handling systems available. Bucket elevators are vertical devices that use buckets to lift and discharge grain. Conveyors, on the other hand, are horizontal or inclined systems that move grain from one point to another. Augers are machinery used to transport grain using a spiral-shaped screw. Lastly, there are other systems that may include equipment like grain cleaners, dryers, and weighing scales, enhancing the overall grain handling process.</p></p>
