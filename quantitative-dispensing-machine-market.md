<p><p>Volumetric dispensing machines are a type of quantitative dispensing machines that measure and dispense fluids based on volume. On the other hand, weight type dispensing machines weigh the substance to be dispensed, ensuring precise measurements. Both types of machines are commonly used in various industries, such as pharmaceutical, food and beverage, and chemical sectors, where accurate and controlled dispensing of liquids is crucial for manufacturing processes.</p></p>
