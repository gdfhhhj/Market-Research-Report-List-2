<p><p>The PTFE venting membrane market includes two types: adhesive vents and vents without backing material. Adhesive vents are equipped with an adhesive backing that allows easy attachment to various surfaces. These vents enable air and gases to escape, while preventing the entry of dust, moisture, and other contaminants. On the other hand, vents without backing material do not have an adhesive layer and are generally used in applications where the vent can be placed between two components without the need for adhesive bonding. They also provide effective venting capabilities.</p></p>
