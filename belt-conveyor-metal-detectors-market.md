<p><p>Belt conveyor metal detectors are used to detect metal contaminants in products that are transported on a conveyor belt. There are two types of belt conveyor metal detectors: flat metal detectors and pipeline metal detectors. Flat metal detectors are installed directly on the conveyor belt and can detect metal contaminants on the surface of the product. Pipeline metal detectors, on the other hand, are installed in a pipeline system and can detect metal contaminants within the product itself as it flows through the pipeline.</p></p>
