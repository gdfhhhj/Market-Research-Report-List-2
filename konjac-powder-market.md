<p><p>Konjac powder is available in two main market types: ordinary konjac powder and purified konjac powder. Ordinary konjac powder refers to the standard form of the powder, which contains the natural composition and properties of konjac. On the other hand, purified konjac powder refers to the refined version, where impurities are removed to obtain a higher concentration of glucomannan, the main active ingredient in konjac. Purified konjac powder is often used for dietary supplements, while ordinary konjac powder has a wider range of applications, including as a thickening agent in food products.</p></p>
