<p><p>AMOLED panels are a type of display technology used in electronic devices. There are two main types - flexible AMOLED panels and rigid AMOLED panels. Flexible AMOLED panels can be bent and curved, allowing for more design flexibility in devices like smartphones and wearables. Rigid AMOLED panels, on the other hand, are not flexible and are commonly used in larger displays like televisions. Both types offer high-quality visuals and vibrant colors, but flexible panels provide additional versatility in terms of form factor.</p></p>
