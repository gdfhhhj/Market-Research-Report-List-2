<p><p>The pre-fabricated cleanroom market can be classified into two types: pre-fabricated softwall cleanrooms and pre-fabricated hardwall cleanrooms. Pre-fabricated softwall cleanrooms are flexible, lightweight structures made of non-rigid materials, such as vinyl curtains. They offer versatility and ease of installation and can be easily expanded or relocated. On the other hand, pre-fabricated hardwall cleanrooms are constructed using rigid materials like aluminum or stainless steel panels, providing a more permanent and durable solution. They offer superior cleanliness and protection against contaminants, making them suitable for environments with stringent cleanliness requirements.</p></p>
