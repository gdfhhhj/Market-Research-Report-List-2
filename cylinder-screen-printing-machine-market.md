<p><p>Cylinder screen printing machines are categorized into three types based on their level of automation: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. Manual machines require operators to perform every step of the printing process, from loading the substrate to lifting the screen and pushing the squeegee. Semi-automatic machines automate some steps, such as screen lifting or substrate loading, whilst still requiring manual intervention for other tasks. Fully automatic machines have complete automation, with the ability to perform all printing tasks independently, significantly improving productivity and reducing the need for manual labor.</p></p>
