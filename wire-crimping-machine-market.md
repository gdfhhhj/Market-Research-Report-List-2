<p><p>The wire crimping machine market is classified into two types: fully automatic wire crimping machines and semi-automatic wire crimping machines. Fully automatic wire crimping machines are designed to perform crimping tasks without human intervention. These machines are equipped with advanced features such as automatic wire feeding and cutting, eliminating the need for manual wire preparation. On the other hand, semi-automatic wire crimping machines require some level of human interaction during the crimping process. They typically involve manual wire insertion and pressing of a foot pedal to initiate crimping.</p></p>
