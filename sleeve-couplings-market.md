<p><p>Sleeve couplings are mechanical devices used to connect two rotating shafts in order to transmit torque between them. They consist of a hollow cylinder called a sleeve with an internally and externally splined surface. The splines on the inside of the sleeve fit with the splines on the shaft, while the splines on the outside fit with the mating splines on the adjacent shaft. This design allows for the transmission of torque while also accommodating the misalignment or axial movement between the shafts.</p><p>The sleeve couplings market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. The increasing demand for power transmission systems in various industries, such as automotive, manufacturing, and mining, is one of the key factors driving the market growth. The growing emphasis on efficient and reliable power transmission systems is further propelling the demand for sleeve couplings.</p><p>Moreover, the rising popularity of flexible couplings, which offer advantages such as shock absorption, vibration dampening, and misalignment compensation, is also contributing to the market growth. Sleeve couplings are widely used as flexible couplings due to their ability to accommodate misalignment between shafts.</p><p>Additionally, the market is witnessing the introduction of advanced sleeve couplings with enhanced features. Manufacturers are focusing on developing couplings with improved load-bearing capacity, increased durability, and higher torque transmission capabilities. These technological advancements are expected to drive the market further.</p><p>Furthermore, the increasing adoption of sleeve couplings in emerging economies, such as India, China, and Brazil, due to the rapid industrialization and infrastructure development, presents lucrative opportunities for market growth.</p><p>In conclusion, the sleeve couplings market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.30% during the forecast period.
