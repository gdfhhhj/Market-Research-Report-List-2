<p><p>The floating parquet floor market offers two main types: engineered and solid. Engineered floating parquet floors consist of a thin veneer of hardwood adhered to a plywood or high-density fiberboard (HDF) core. This type provides stability, resistance to moisture, and affordability. On the other hand, solid floating parquet floors are made entirely of solid hardwood and offer durability, a traditional aesthetic, and the ability to be sanded and refinished multiple times. Both types cater to different preferences and needs, allowing customers to choose the most suitable option for their specific requirements.</p></p>
