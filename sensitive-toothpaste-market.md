Additionally, sensitive toothpaste often contains fluoride to strengthen tooth enamel and promote oral health.</p><p>The sensitive toothpaste market has witnessed significant growth in recent years due to increasing dental problems and a growing awareness of oral health among consumers. Factors such as excessive consumption of acidic foods and beverages, tooth grinding, gum recession, and poor oral hygiene contribute to the increasing number of individuals experiencing tooth sensitivity. As a result, the demand for sensitive toothpaste has surged, leading to market expansion.</p><p>Furthermore, an increasing focus on oral healthcare has propelled the growth of the market. People are becoming more conscious of their dental health and are actively seeking products that address specific dental issues.
