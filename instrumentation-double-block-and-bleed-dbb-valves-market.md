<p><p>The Instrumentation Double Block and Bleed (DBB) Valves market consists of four types: Single Block, Single Block and Bleed, Double Block, and Double Block and Bleed. Single Block valves provide isolation in one direction, Single Block and Bleed valves provide isolation in one direction and a bleed valve for monitoring or draining, Double Block valves provide isolation in both directions, and Double Block and Bleed valves provide isolation in both directions and a bleed valve. These valves are used in various industries to ensure safety, control fluid flow, and prevent leaks.</p></p>
