<p><p>The spectacle frame market is classified into three main types: full rim frames, half-rim frames, and rimless frames. Full rim frames encircle the lenses entirely, providing maximum support and durability. Half-rim frames offer a more stylish and lightweight alternative, as they only partially enclose the lenses. Rimless frames, as the name suggests, have no rim around the lenses, providing a minimalistic and barely-there aesthetic. Each type caters to different preferences and needs, allowing consumers to choose the style that best suits their personal taste and comfort.</p></p>
