Monitor support arms are designed to hold and position monitors for surgeons to easily view during procedures. Camera support arms secure cameras for capturing surgical procedures and transmitting live video to screens. Lamp support arms are used to hold and adjust surgical lamps, providing optimal lighting in the operating room. These support arms play crucial roles in enhancing surgical precision, efficiency, and safety.</p></p>
