<p><p>The biotechnological enzyme market comprises various types of enzymes, including hydrolases, lyases, isomerases, ligases, and others. Hydrolases are enzymes that catalyze the breakdown of chemical bonds through hydrolysis. Lyases, on the other hand, facilitate the removal of chemical groups without the presence of water. Isomerases help in the rearrangement of molecular structures, while ligases assist in joining two molecules together. The "others" category includes enzymes with various functions, such as oxidoreductases and transferases. All these enzyme types play significant roles in different biotechnological applications, ranging from food production to medicine.</p></p>
