<p><p>Electric Discharge Machines (EDMs) are specialized tools used in manufacturing processes to shape and cut metal parts. The market for EDMs can be divided into two main types: the Electric Spark Forming Machine and the EDM Wire Cutting Machine. Electric Spark Forming Machines use electrical discharges to gradually erode metal, creating desired shapes. On the other hand, EDM Wire Cutting Machines use a thin, electrically-charged wire to accurately cut through metal. Both these machines offer precise and efficient options for metalworking applications, making them important tools in various industries.</p></p>
