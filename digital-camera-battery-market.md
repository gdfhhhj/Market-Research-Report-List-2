<p><strong>The Digital Camera Battery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ni-MH</li><li>Li-ion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital camera battery market primarily consists of two types: Ni-MH (Nickel-Metal Hydride) and Li-ion (Lithium-ion). Ni-MH batteries are rechargeable and widely used due to their moderate capacity and affordability. However, they suffer from a memory effect, reducing their overall lifespan. On the other hand, Li-ion batteries are more advanced, offering higher energy density, longer lifespan, and no memory effect. Although Li-ion batteries are pricier, their superior performance, lightweight design, and ability to hold a charge for longer periods make them the preferred choice in the digital camera battery market.</p></p>
