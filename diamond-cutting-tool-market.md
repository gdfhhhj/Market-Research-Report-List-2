<p><strong>The Diamond Cutting Tool Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solid</li><li>Indexable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The diamond cutting tool market is divided into two main types: solid and indexable. Solid diamond cutting tools are made from a single piece of diamond material and are mainly used for precision cutting applications. On the other hand, indexable diamond cutting tools consist of a diamond insert that can be replaced or indexed when it becomes worn out or dulled. This type of tool offers cost-effectiveness and convenience as it eliminates the need for replacing the entire tool.</p></p>
