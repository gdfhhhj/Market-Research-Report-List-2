For instance, some EPDs now come with built-in artificial intelligence capabilities, which allow for real-time monitoring of radiation exposure levels and provide instant alerts and warnings to the user.</p><p>Another trend in the EPD market is the miniaturization of these devices. Manufacturers are focusing on developing compact and lightweight EPDs, ensuring that they are comfortable for workers to wear for extended periods. This trend is driven by the increasing demand for wearable technologies in various industries.</p><p>The EPD market is also witnessing the advent of wireless connectivity features. It also enhances the overall efficiency of radiation safety programs by providing accurate and up-to-date information.</p><p>In conclusion, the EPD market is experiencing steady growth, driven by factors such as increasing awareness about radiation safety, technological advancements, and the need for real-time monitoring.
