<p><p>Rubber Coated Fabrics are a type of material that has a layer of rubber applied to one or both sides. There are two main types of Rubber Coated Fabrics in the market: Natural Rubber Coated Fabrics and Synthetic Rubber Coated Fabrics. Natural Rubber Coated Fabrics are made from natural rubber, which is derived from trees. They offer good flexibility, high tensile strength, and excellent resistance to abrasion and tearing. On the other hand, Synthetic Rubber Coated Fabrics are made from synthetic rubber, which is chemically synthesized. They provide similar properties as natural rubber but may vary in terms of cost, durability, and specific applications.</p></p>
