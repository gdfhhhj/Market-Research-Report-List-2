<p><p>Scanning Probe Microscopes (SPM) are high-resolution imaging devices used in scientific research and industry. Atomic Force Microscopes (AFM) utilize a sharp probe to scan the sample surface, detecting atomic forces and providing detailed topographic information. Laser Force Microscopes (LFM) use laser beams to measure forces on a sample surface, enabling the study of mechanical properties and interactions. Magnetic Force Microscopes (MFM) employ magnetic probes to map magnetic fields at the nanoscale level. These diverse SPM types offer valuable capabilities for studying various materials and phenomena, aiding scientific advancements and technological developments.</p></p>
