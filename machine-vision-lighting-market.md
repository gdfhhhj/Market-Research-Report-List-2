They are widely used in machine vision applications due to their long lifespan and low power consumption.</p><p>2. Halogen Lighting: Halogen lights provide a bright and focused light source. They are commonly used in machine vision systems that require a continuous and intense light output.</p><p>3. Xenon Lighting: Xenon lights offer a broad spectrum of light that closely resembles natural sunlight. They are suitable for applications that require accurate color rendition and high-speed imaging.</p><p>4. Fluorescent Lighting: Fluorescent lights emit a steady and uniform light source. They are commonly used in machine vision applications that require consistent illumination over a large area.</p></p>
