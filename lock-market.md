<p><p>The lock market offers various types of locks to cater to different security needs. Padlocks are portable locks that can be affixed to an object using a shackle. Deadbolts provide high-level security for external doors with a bolt that extends into the door frame. Knob locks are commonly found on internal doors, but provide minimal security. Lever handle locks offer ease of use and are commonly used in commercial settings. Cam locks are often used for cabinets and drawers. Lastly, other types of locks include mortise locks, rim locks, and combination locks, each with its own features and applications.</p></p>
