<p><p>Silicon-based masking tapes are adhesive tapes primarily used for masking and protection during painting, coating, or other surface treatments. The market offers two main types: single side and double side tapes. Single side masking tapes have adhesive on one side and a non-adhesive surface on the other. They are commonly used for general masking applications. On the other hand, double side masking tapes have adhesive on both sides and are ideal for bonding or mounting applications where two surfaces need to be joined securely.</p></p>
