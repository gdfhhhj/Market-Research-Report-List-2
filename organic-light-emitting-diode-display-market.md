<p><p>The Organic Light Emitting Diode (OLED) display market includes three major types: Transparent OLED, Stacked OLED, and Inverted OLED. Transparent OLED allows light to pass through, making it suitable for applications like heads-up displays. Stacked OLED consists of multiple OLED layers that enhance brightness and color gamut, making it ideal for high-quality image display. Inverted OLED architecture reverses the positions of the anode and cathode, providing better efficiency and longer lifespan. These different types cater to various display requirements and offer unique advantages in terms of transparency, picture quality, and power efficiency.</p></p>
