<p><p>Specialty Grade DWP refers to high-quality wood pulp used in various industries. Alkali Wood Pulp, also known as Sulfate Wood Pulp, is produced using the kraft process, which involves cooking wood chips in a solution containing sodium hydroxide and sodium sulfide. This type of pulp is primarily used in the production of paper and paperboard. On the other hand, Wood Pulp of Sulfite is manufactured using the sulfite process, wherein wood chips are treated with sulfurous acid. This pulp finds application in specialty papers, rayon, and cellulose derivatives.</p></p>
