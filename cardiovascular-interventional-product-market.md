<p><p>The cardiovascular interventional product market consists of various types of medical devices that are used in the treatment and management of cardiovascular diseases. The market is segmented into four major categories: coronary stents, PTCA balloon catheters, guide wires, and other products. Coronary stents are small mesh tubes inserted into narrowed coronary arteries to improve blood flow. PTCA balloon catheters are used to open blocked arteries by inflating a balloon and releasing the plaque. Guide wires are thin, flexible wires used to guide other devices into blood vessels. Other products include a range of devices like embolic protection devices, atherectomy devices, and vascular closure devices.</p></p>
