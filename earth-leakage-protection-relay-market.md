<p><p>The Earth Leakage Protection Relay market consists of two types, namely Manual Reset and Auto Reset. In the Manual Reset market, the relay needs to be manually reset after tripping due to an earth leakage fault. This allows the user to investigate the cause of the fault and take appropriate action. On the other hand, in the Auto Reset market, the relay automatically resets itself after tripping, eliminating the need for manual intervention. This ensures continuous protection without the risk of human error or delay in resetting the relay.</p></p>
